Frequently Asked Questions

Can you swim with dolphins in 30A?

Seize the extraordinary chance to swim with wild dolphins in Panama City Beach (near Destin and 30A) during the four-hour dolphin swim tour, available from February 17 through December 15, contingent upon weather conditions and availability.

Do dolphins like swimming with humans?

Dolphins do not engage in activities like swimming with people, "kissing" people, or towing people through the water due to preference; they do so out of necessity, as these are not natural behaviors, and captive dolphins are trained to perform them correctly to ensure they receive food.

What is the difference between dolphin discovery and dolphin encounter?

At this location, you can select from two programs, "Dolphin Encounter" and "Dolphin Discovery," both offering the opportunity for a close and personal experience with affectionate Indo-Pacific bottlenose dolphins, with the only distinction being whether you will enter the water with them or not.

Can you swim with dolphins in Destin?

For an unforgettable experience in Destin, don't miss the chance to swim with dolphins, with Miramar Beach, just a short distance away, providing the perfect setting to connect with these intelligent and playful creatures.
